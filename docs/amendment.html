Amendment Text

A proposed constitutional amendment to limit Presidential Pardon powers and enforce accountability.

Proposed Amendment

Section 1. The pardon power of the President shall not extend to any person who, at the time of the conduct for which pardon is sought:

(a) held an office of the United States whose appointment required confirmation by the Senate;

(b) served in an acting, interim, or temporary capacity in any such office; or

(c) served in the Executive Office of the President or in any successor office designated by Congress, regardless of title or organizational form.

Section 2.Every pardon shall:

(a) identify the individual by legal name;

(b) specify the conduct pardoned; and

(c) identify the federal statute or statues, including section numbers, to which the pardon applies; and

(d) shall apply only to the offenses expressly identified and to no other offense, whether related or arising from the same conduct.

No pardon may use general, omnibus, or open-ended language such as, but not limited to, “all offenses against the United States.”

Section 3. No Self-Amnesty and No Class Pardons

No President may pardon himself or herself. No pardon may be issued to any class of persons defined by office, agency, function, or status.

Section 4. Any pardon issued in violation of this Amendment shall have no legal force.

Section 5.Nothing in this Amendment shall restrict the President’s power to grant pardons to persons not described in Section 1, except as limited by the requirements of Section 2.

Section 6.This Amendment shall take effect upon ratification.

Notes on Construction

This text is drafted to do six things plainly:

  • Limit the President's ability to pardon those who commit crimes on behalf of the Executive.
  • Make pardons individualize and specific.
  • The President cannot pardon himself or herself.
  • Make illegitimate any pardon issued in contradiction to this amendment.
  • Reiterate that the pardon power is otherwise untouched.
  • Make certain the amendment takes effect immediately.
